First Trilogy

Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace (1999)

A young boy with exceptional potential is discovered on a desert planet, while the Republic faces political turmoil and the threat of a mysterious Sith Lord. The film introduces the origins of key characters and sets the stage for the galactic conflict.

Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones (2002)

Amid rising tensions across the galaxy, a Jedi Knight and his apprentice uncover a sinister plot involving a massive army and escalating dangers. This episode delves into forbidden relationships and the seeds of betrayal.

Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith (2005)

The galaxy plunges into chaos as the Republic faces internal corruption and war. A fateful transformation occurs as the dark side's influence reaches its peak, leading to the fall of the Jedi and the rise of an empire.
